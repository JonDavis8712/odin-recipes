Tasty Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is an italian classic pasta dish. Made on very wid flat sheets of pasta, served as cut squares fresh out of the oven. The dish dates abk to as far as 1282, being referenced in a poem in memoriali bolognesi("Bolognesi Memorials") since then, there have been many itterations of the recipe, all of which fall into a particular category of flat stacked pasta dishes.

RECIPE

  • 12 lasagna noobles uncooked
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese shredded and divided
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound italian sausage
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped
  • 1 egg beated

STEPS

  1. preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. unroll cresecnt sheets on a lightly floured surface. Cut out the triangles into 3 smaller triangles
  3. place a cocktail weiner on the thick side of each triangle and gently roll to thinner side
  4. transfer over to a medium backing sheet, brush with the melted butter and sprinkle with coarse salt
  5. place in the oven and bake until golden brown, about 12 to 15 minutes
