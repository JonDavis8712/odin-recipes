Tasty Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is an italian classic pasta dish. Made on very wid flat sheets of pasta, served as cut squares fresh out of the oven. The dish dates abk to as far as 1282, being referenced in a poem in memoriali bolognesi("Bolognesi Memorials") since then, there have been many itterations of the recipe, all of which fall into a particular category of flat stacked pasta dishes.

RECIPE

  • 12 lasagna noobles uncooked
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese shredded and divided
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound italian sausage
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped
  • 1 egg beated

STEPS

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cook beef, sausage, onion and garlic over medium high heat, until no pink remains. Drain the fat
  3. stir in pasta sauce, tomato paste, italian seasoning. Let simmer for 5 minutes
  4. make the cheese mixture by combining 1 1/2 cups mozzarella, 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, ricotta, parsley, and egg
  5. add 1 cup of meat sauce to a 9x13 pan. Top with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with 1/3 of the cheese mixture and 1 cup meat sauce. Repeat twice more. Finish with 3 nooles topped with remaining sauce
  6. cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes
  7. uncover, sprinkle with remaining cheese (2 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup parmesan) and bake an additional 15 minutes or until browned and bubbly. Broil 2-3 minutes if desired
  8. rest 10 to 15 minutes before cutting
